Forums

Forumns are for a single tag only, groups can be formed for multiple tags. For example: dogs, Seattle, and pet owners would be 3 differnt forums. Dog owners of Seattle would be a group with multiple tags.

As a user, I can view forums that match my interests.

As a user, I can add/edit/delete forum topics/comments.

As a user, I can view/like/follow someone else's topic/thread.
